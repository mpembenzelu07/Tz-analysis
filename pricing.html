Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Can I switch plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get prorated credit for your current plan.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You get full access to all features during the trial.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.
Do you offer discounts for academies?
Yes, we offer special educational pricing for registered youth academies and non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team for details.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time. No long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
Do you offer volume discounts?
Yes, for clubs with multiple teams or football associations, we offer volume discounts. Contact our enterprise sales team for custom pricing.
