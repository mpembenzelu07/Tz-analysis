Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does analysis take?
Analysis typically takes 5-10 minutes for basic data uploads. For video analysis or large datasets, it may take up to 30 minutes. You'll receive an email notification when your analysis is complete.
What file formats do you support?
We support CSV, Excel (xlsx, xls), JSON (Opta/StatsBomb format), and video files (MP4, MOV). For tracking data, we support various GPS formats including GPX and FIT files.
Can I try before I buy?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start. You can analyze up to 3 teams during the trial period.
How accurate is the analysis?
Our analysis has a 94% accuracy rate based on coach feedback. The accuracy depends on the quality and quantity of data provided. We recommend uploading data from at least 4 matches for best results.
Can I export my data?
Yes, you can export all your analysis data in multiple formats including CSV for raw data, PDF for reports, and PowerPoint for presentations. Professional and Enterprise plans also offer API access.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption (AES-256), secure data centers with SOC 2 compliance, and comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations. Your data is never shared with third parties.
