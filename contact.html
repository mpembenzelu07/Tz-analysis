Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does analysis take?
Analysis typically takes 5-10 minutes for basic data uploads. For video analysis or large datasets, it may take up to 30 minutes.
What file formats do you support?
We support CSV, Excel (xlsx, xls), JSON (Opta/StatsBomb format), and video files (MP4, MOV). For tracking data, we support various GPS formats.
Can I try before I buy?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.
Do you offer discounts for academies?
We offer special pricing for youth academies, educational institutions, and non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team for details.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure data centers, and comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations.
Can I export my data?
Yes, you can export all your analysis data in multiple formats including CSV, PDF, and PowerPoint presentations.
