Our Journey

2019

The Beginning

Founded by former professional analysts who saw the gap between elite and grassroots football technology. Started as a research project at Stanford University.

2020

First Prototype

Developed our first MVP with 5 beta teams. Learned that coaches needed simpler interfaces and more actionable insights, not just raw data.

2021

Seed Funding

Raised $2M in seed funding to expand the team and develop our proprietary AI algorithms for tactical pattern recognition.

2022

Public Launch

Launched FootIntel Pro to the public. Onboarded our first 100 paying customers, including youth academies and semi-professional clubs.

2023

Global Expansion

Expanded to 42 countries. Partnered with football associations in Africa and South America to adapt our platform for developing football markets.

2024

Today & Beyond

Serving 500+ teams worldwide. Continuously improving our AI models and adding new features based on coach feedback and football innovation.
